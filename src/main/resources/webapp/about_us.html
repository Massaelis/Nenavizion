about us:

Nenavizion, is a fashion brand, founded in 2021 in Athens, Greece, started by two visionary 20-year-old friends. Originating from the sale of dead stock eyewear from the 2000s. Later on the brand swiftly expanded into adding jewelry (mainly crosses ), as well as shoes and clothing pieces.

The name "nenavizion" is a fusion of a Russian term, "ненавижу" (nenavizhu), translating to "hate," and "Vizion" (with a distinctive "z"), encapsulating a profound meaning.

This brand is more than a label; it embodies a mindset determined to revolutionize Greek fashion and, subsequently, make a global impact. Nenavizion thrives on collaboration, embracing the synergy of like-minded young artists who share our vision. So, if you would like to be part of the team feel free to contact us ;

Embarking on this venture with a budget of 30 euros and no prior industry knowledge. We relied on pure vision and an unwavering love for the youth and fashion community.

To those who harbor a vision. Trust your instincts. Nurture your curiosity. And let inspiration guide you. Unleash your creativity and express love unabashedly.
